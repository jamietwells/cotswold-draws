About

The South Cotswolds Arts Network strives to make a positive impact by providing grants to good causes in the arts community of Gloucestershire and Wiltshire. We're passionate about fostering cultural participation, advancing arts education, and promoting social engagement and wellbeing. We believe in the power of the arts to bring people together. That's why we extend our support not only to traditional charities but also to non-profits and community groups that share our vision of a thriving arts sector. To achieve our goals, our Board has the power to raise funds, receive donations, collaborate with like-minded organisations, and take any lawful steps necessary to fulfill our mission.
