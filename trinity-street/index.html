Trinity Street Communications Ltd

Trinity Street Communications Ltd is a campaigning and fundraising consultancy, with a special emphasis on developing and leading first class political campaigns across the English West Country.

We are based in Tetbury, Gloucestershire and our current and former clients include the Green Party, the Liberal Democrats and several smaller parties and Independents.

As a fundraising, marketing and communications consultancy, we exemplify excellence in delivering outstanding and insightful campaigns and services. We thrive on the principles of independence and innovation, guaranteeing our clients unparalleled solutions and electoral success.

2024 is set to be a landmark year in British politics, perhaps the most exciting general election in a generation. We are here to support your campaigning, whether you are a candidate, a political party, a charitable body or any other organisation.

Our Director, Chris Twells, was elected as the new District Councillor for Tetbury with Upton ward in May 2023, defeating the then Mayor of Tetbury, a Conservative. His victory made headlines across the world – from Glasgow to Ghana – and was highlighted on BBC One’s Have I Got News For You.

Chris sits as a Tetbury & District Independent on Cotswold District Council and does not take a party whip.

Chris studied Law at the University of York and has worked in the political and charitable sectors since graduating in 2012. He takes a keen interest in constitutional and electoral law. Chris has twelve years’ experience as a professional political campaigner and community organiser, running successful campaigns in some of the most deprived areas of England.
